## Key improvement

The expanded corpus gives the advisor **multiple perspectives** on every life question — not just the Gita's philosophical view, but:
- **Practical wisdom** from Arthashastra and Panchatantra
- **Emotional depth** from Yoga Vasistha's stories
- **Cross-tradition** insight from Dhammapada
- **Exact verse citations** from Mahabharata with Sanskrit + translation
- **Counterpoints** (Arthashastra on anger as righteous vs Dhammapada on releasing it)

The advisor can now tailor responses to the seeker's temperament — a pragmatist gets Arthashastra, a philosopher gets Yoga Vasistha, a devotee gets Markandeya Purana.
